Questions

What happens to my history on the free tier?

Your chat history lives in your browser. When you close the app, it clears. Upgrade to Memory to keep it.

Can I export my data?

Yes — Memory subscribers can export their full history at any time from settings. Your data is yours.

What is a signal?

A signal is a presence pulse from your AI — no words, just contact. It arrives as a notification in the app.

Do I need special hardware?

No. PulseBeat works entirely through the app.
